# Swissphone Classic Archive

**Preserving Swissphone history — one measurement at a time.**

Open documentation and reverse engineering of classic Swissphone pagers, charging cradles, programming accessories and related software.

## Scope

The archive is intentionally not limited to the Memo. Planned device families include Memo, Joker, Quattro, Boss, Patron, RE329, RE429, RE529 and related accessories.

## Method

- Separate **verified findings** from **working hypotheses**.
- Preserve raw measurement files.
- Document equipment, wiring, sample rate and procedure.
- Correct errors transparently.
- Do not upload copyrighted software or manuals without permission.

## Current focus: Swissphone Memo

Confirmed observations include a quartz marked `QS421 / 20.94 / TEW5E`, RF/oscillator peaks near 20.99 MHz and harmonics, and approximately 3 V idle levels on pager contacts Pin 2 and Pin 3. Logic-analyzer captures are stored under `measurements/logic-analyzer/`.
