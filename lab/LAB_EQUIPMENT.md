# Laboratory Equipment

- TinySA and TinySA Ultra
- Rigol mixed-signal oscilloscope with FFT
- Kingst LA5032 logic analyzer
- XGecu TL866 II Plus programmer
- XEAST XE-702S multimeter
- Microscope with integrated camera
- Near-field probes / self-built probe
- USB-UART adapters

The TL866 II Plus may become useful if a supported external EEPROM, flash or programmable logic device is identified. It should not be connected blindly to an in-circuit device.
